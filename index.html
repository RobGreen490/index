Welcome to Taniti

Taniti is a picturesque, tropical island nestled in the heart of the Pacific, offering visitors a diverse landscape that includes sandy and rocky beaches, lush rainforests, and a stunning mountainous interior, home to a small, active volcano. Spanning less than 500 square miles, this island is rich in natural beauty and culture, with an indigenous population of around 20,000. Once primarily centered around fishing and agriculture, Taniti's economy has seen a shift with the rise in tourism, bringing a fresh vibrancy to this tranquil island paradise. Tourist can enjoy a wide range of activities.


Activities such as

  • Seeking adventure in the rainforest.
  • Relaxation on pristine beaches.
  • Explore unique volcanic terrain.

As a growing tourist destination, Taniti offers a wide array of attractions and amenities. Visitors can indulge in local seafood at one of the island's 10 restaurants, enjoy diverse activities such as snorkeling, zip-lining, or chartered fishing tours, and take in the beauty of Taniti City's native architecture and surrounding beaches. Transportation is convenient, with public buses, taxis, and rental bikes available, making it easy to explore the island's diverse sights. With a variety of lodging options ranging from budget-friendly hostels to a luxurious four-star resort, Taniti ensures a comfortable stay for all types of travelers. Whether you're here for a peaceful getaway or an action-packed adventure, Taniti provides an unforgettable experience in a stunning, tropical setting.
