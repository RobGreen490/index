Transportation

Flying to Taniti offers the fastest and most efficient way to reach the island, with direct flights landing at the small airport that accommodates both small jets and propeller planes. It's ideal for those looking to maximize their time on the island, as flights typically take only a few hours. Additionally, the airport is expanding to accommodate larger jets in the near future, making air travel even more accessible.

Alternatively, arriving by cruise provides a leisurely and scenic approach to the island. The cruise docks in Yellow Leaf Bay once a week, allowing visitors to enjoy the relaxed pace of a sea voyage before exploring Taniti's stunning coastline. While it takes longer than flying, the cruise offers a unique experience, with picturesque views of the Pacific and an opportunity to enjoy the open sea before stepping onto the island.
